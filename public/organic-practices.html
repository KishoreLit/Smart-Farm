Natural methods for a sustainable and healthy farm

1. Composting

Composting is the process of converting organic waste into nutrient-rich fertilizer. It improves soil structure, boosts microbial activity, and reduces the need for chemical inputs.

2. Natural Pest Control

Organic farmers use neem oil, garlic spray, or companion planting to control pests without harming the environment or human health.

3. Crop Rotation

Rotating crops each season helps prevent soil depletion, controls pests and diseases, and improves yield naturally.

4. Green Manuring

Green manure crops like sunhemp or clover are grown and then plowed back into the soil to enhance fertility and organic matter content.

5. Biofertilizers

Biofertilizers like Rhizobium, Azospirillum, and mycorrhizae improve nutrient availability in a natural and eco-friendly way.
