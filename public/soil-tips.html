Improve fertility, boost yields, and farm sustainably

1. Use Organic Matter

Incorporate compost or manure into your soil regularly to improve structure and provide essential nutrients.

2. Rotate Crops

Crop rotation helps prevent nutrient depletion and reduces pest and disease buildup in the soil.

3. Cover Cropping

Planting cover crops like legumes prevents erosion and adds nitrogen naturally to the soil.

4. Avoid Over-Tilling

Excessive tilling can damage soil structure and kill beneficial organisms. Use minimal or no-till methods when possible.

5. Conduct Soil Testing

Regularly test your soil to understand nutrient levels and pH, so you can apply the right fertilizers.

6. Use Biofertilizers

Biofertilizers such as Rhizobium and Azotobacter enhance nutrient uptake and promote healthy plant growth naturally.
